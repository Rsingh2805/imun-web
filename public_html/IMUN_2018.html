IMUN THAILAND 2018

June 1 - 4th, 2018

The first International Model United Nations Conference was held from June 1st to June 4th, 2018 at Bangkok Palace Hotel, Thailand. Our committee members are comprised of people who are carefully selected based on their extensive model UN and debate experience. Moreover, we had more than 250 delegates from various countries who experienced the thrills and challenges in an IMUN Conference by stepping into the shoes of UN representatives and decision makers around the world together with the IMUN organizing committee.

The conference featured intensive training workshops taught by experienced chairs, briefings on important agenda items and a conference session simulating 5 different committees : United Nations Security Council (UNSC), International Court of Justice (ICJ), World Health Organization (WHO), United Nations Environment Programme (UNEP) and United Nations Human Rights Council (UNHRC). Delegates at IMUN are immersed in a thriving fast-paced environment, working together to create practical and dynamic solutions to the world’s current crises. This allows them to think critically, to collaborate with others, to propose constructive resolutions, and to develop the crucial diplomatic skills.

Speakers

SANTA NIBEDITA

She is the Co-founder of WASHable International, she presented it to David Nabarro , Advisor to Secretary-General regarding UN SDGs’16. She has been selected for Merit 360 2016 at UN Headquarters in New York , Stanford ASES Summit’17 at Stanford University and MIT Entrepreneurship Bootcamp 2017 in Australia.

ELVANA SHALA

She is the ambassador of Goodwill for Kosovo and a successful representation of Kosovo in various conferences, summits and various public diplomacy events in countries such as New York, London, Dubai, Geneva, Turkey, Morocco, Prague etc. from 2014 to 2017 and has been awarded with the “Laureates in Social Welfare” in 2015 and "Best Poet of the City of Peja" in 2006.

NINDYA MIESYE AGITA PASARIBU

She is a prominent Indonesian figure in involvement of youth for social change and has been selected as Southeast Asian Young Leader for the IISS-Shangri-La Dialogue in Singapore. She has been offered National Blue Ocean Strategy as Community Manager and Social Media Strategist by Ministry of Finance of Malaysia.

DANA GRABER LADEK

She is the Chief of mission at IOM Thailand and an International professional with over 20 years of work experience in humanitarian aid, development programming and program management in coordination with various governmental agencies, UN amongst others.
