IMUN CHINA 2019

IMUN has always strived to be a platform for youth to voice out their opinions dynamically. With an aim of empowering the future leaders, IMUN has successfully completed conferences in various parts of the world. In this path of harmonising youth to come together and collectively work for a better tomorrow, IMUN has primed the youth to effectively handle the global issues. To continue it’s legacy, IMUN is back with it’s China edition, which will be conducted in the city of Nanjing. IMUN CHINA 2019 in collaboration with NKMUN is going to be a wonderful experience for all MUN enthusiasts.

IMUN for long has been an archetype for MUN simulation. Through the medium of simulation, we want to prepare the youth to handle and learn diplomacy and deliberations. IMUN CHINA 2019 in collaboration with NKMUN is driven by an effort to inculcate and foster skills like public speaking, negotiations, research in an international set up. The skills learned at IMUN CHINA 2019 will continue to inspire the youth and sequentially will prepare the youth to be a medium for change, both at a local and global level.

IMUN China 2019

IMUN has achieved many milestones in past conferences. And we hope that the legacy continues. For IMUN CHINA 2019 we are collaborating with one of the biggest MUN, NKUMUN. With this collaboration we hope to bring for all our delegates a unique experience and learnings that will help them comprehensively..

Conference Location

IMUN China 2019 will be held in Nanjing International Expo Centre, which is located in the HeXi CBD in Jianye district and has become iconic new landscape in Nanjing.

Eligibility of IMUN China 2019

  • Only students can participate in IMUN China 2019.
  • Students under age of 21 or age of 21 can participate as a delegate.
  • Students more than age of 21 can participate as an observer.
